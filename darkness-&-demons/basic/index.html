Introduction

What Is Darkness & Demons Basic?

Darkness & Demons Basic is a streamlined fantasy role‑playing game designed to be easy to learn, fast to play, and welcoming to anyone stepping into tabletop adventures for the first time. It keeps the heart of classic d20 fantasy—heroes, monsters, magic, and danger—but presents it in a clear, modern style that avoids complicated rules and heavy jargon. The focus is on smooth play, quick decisions, and rules that support imagination rather than getting in its way.

At its core, the game is about a group of players creating heroes who explore mysterious places, battle fearsome creatures, and make choices that shape the world around them. One player takes the role of the Game Master, describing the world and guiding the story, while the others act as the heroes within it. Every challenge—whether swinging a sword, sneaking past a guard, or casting a spell—is resolved with a simple roll of a twenty‑sided die.

Darkness & Demons Basic uses a clean three‑action turn system and straightforward character abilities, making it easy to understand what your hero can do at any moment. Characters grow from level 1 to level 20, gaining new powers and spells along the way, but the rules stay focused and approachable throughout. Whether you’re coming from Pathfinder or Dungeons & Dragons, or you’ve never played a role‑playing game before, this edition is built to help you jump in quickly and start having fun.

For players who are curious about Pathfinder’s deeper rules, Darkness & Demons Basic also serves as a gentle bridge. Its three‑action economy, level‑based math, and simple proficiency system introduce the core ideas that make Pathfinder tick, but in a lighter, more forgiving form. Once you’re comfortable here, moving into a full Pathfinder game will feel natural and intuitive.

But this system is not just a stepping stone. Darkness & Demons Basic is a complete game in its own right, designed to stand on its own legs. If you enjoy the faster pace, the lighter rules, or the simplicity of play, you can keep adventuring in this system for as long as you like. Many groups will find that the clarity and ease of Basic are exactly what they want from a fantasy RPG—and there’s no need to go anywhere else unless you want to.

How to Use This Page

This webpage is organized to guide you from the basics of play to the full rules of the game in a clear, approachable order. The early chapters explain the core ideas you need to understand how the game works—rolling dice, making checks, taking actions, and leveling up. Later chapters introduce the options you can choose for your character, such as ancestries, classes, skills, equipment, and spells. The final chapters provide the rules for running adventures, resolving combat, and using monsters in your stories.

You do not need to read the entire ruleset before you start playing. Most groups begin by reading the Character Creation section, choosing an ancestry and class, and then learning the rules as they come up during play. The Game Master may want to read ahead into the Combat and GM Rules sections, but even that can be learned gradually. Darkness & Demons Basic is designed so that new players can jump in quickly and discover the game step by step.

Each section is written to stand on its own, so you can easily look up rules during play. Headings, clear examples, and consistent formatting make it simple to find what you need. The sidebar on the left lists every major section and subsection, allowing you to navigate the entire ruleset with a single click.

Whether you are learning your first tabletop role‑playing game or teaching it to others, this page is meant to be a friendly companion at the table. Read what you need, skip what you don’t, and return to any section whenever you want to explore more of what Darkness & Demons Basic has to offer.
